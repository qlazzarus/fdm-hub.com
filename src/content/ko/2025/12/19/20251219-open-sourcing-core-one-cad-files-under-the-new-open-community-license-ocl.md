## Open-sourcing CORE One CAD Files Under the New Open Community License (OCL)
![image](https://storage.googleapis.com/prusa3d-content-prod-14e8-wordpress-blog-prod/2025/12/d6040fa0-opencommunitylicense_blog-698x325.jpg)

The CORE One 3D printer's CAD files have been made available under the Open Community License, allowing users to access, modify, and share the designs. This move aims to foster innovation and collaboration within the 3D printing community, enabling users to customize their printers and contribute to the development of new features.
