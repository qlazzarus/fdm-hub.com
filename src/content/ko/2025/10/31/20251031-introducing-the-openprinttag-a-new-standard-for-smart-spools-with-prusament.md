## The OpenPrintTag Is Here! Launch of the Open-Source Smart Spool Standard
![image](https://storage.googleapis.com/prusa3d-content-prod-14e8-wordpress-blog-prod/2025/10/5bbab75d-opt_launch_blog-698x325.png)

The introduction of the OpenPrintTag marks a significant advancement in 3D printing technology, providing a new standard for smart spools. This initiative is launched alongside the redesigned Prusament spool, enhancing user experience by enabling better tracking and management of filament usage. The open-source nature of the OpenPrintTag allows for broader community involvement and innovation in 3D printing materials.
