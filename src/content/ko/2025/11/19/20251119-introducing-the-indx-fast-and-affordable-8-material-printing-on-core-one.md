## Introducing the INDX: Fast and Affordable 8-Material Printing on CORE One
![image](https://storage.googleapis.com/prusa3d-content-prod-14e8-wordpress-blog-prod/2025/11/ce165586-bondtech_indx_1920x920-3-698x325.jpg)

The INDX is a new 3D printing technology that allows users to print with up to eight different materials simultaneously, enhancing design flexibility and efficiency. Exclusively available on the CORE One platform, this innovation aims to reduce production costs and time, making it an attractive option for both hobbyists and professionals in the 3D printing community.
